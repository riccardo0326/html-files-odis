8. ODIS Creator Preparation Process

The preparation process prepares the released editorial objects (diagnostic packages (production, rollout and test), hotfixes, EU5 export packages) in the Mirror Server 2 depot for customer distribution. A detailed description is given below for packages in which distribution is managed differently. The settings in the process are brand-specific and are managed by the location administrators.
