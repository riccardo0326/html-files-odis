8.11. Cooperation export

A collaboration export is a collection of GFF data from ODIS Creator that is needed for collaboration with other manufacturers who do not have access to ODIS Creator. Collected GFF data will be converted into HTML format during a collaboration export so that collaborative partners can view them in a browser.

The view for creating and viewing collaboration exports is divided into three different displays (see Figure 624, “Collaboration export display”). The navigation tree and the properties display on the left side, the editor to define the collaboration exports on the right side. The displays are described in more detail in the following.

Figure 624. Collaboration export display


The results from collaboration exports are automatically stored in a central, defined folder. The results in the central folder are deleted if possible when the cooperation export is deleted in OC.
