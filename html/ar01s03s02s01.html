3.2.1. Views Bar

All views for a specific topic are combined in ODIS Creator in views(for example Figure 24, “Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator”). The individual views are opened through the buttons in the view bar (such as 1a and 1b).

In the open views bar, all selectable action elements to open the individual navigators are displayed.

In the order management view (1a), you will find the action elements to display, for example, tasks and orders (see Section 6, “ODIS Creator Order Management Process”). If the (Orders action element 2) is selected, the order navigator (3) is opened on the right side where the current orders are displayed.

Figure 24. Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator


You usually do not have to assume all tasks that can be performed in ODIS Creator. Therefore you will only see the views that are required for your work and research, and that correspond to your role in the system and the rights associated with that role.

If you switch to a different view, the editors in the view you exited will close. If you have not saved your entries yet, ODIS Creator will ask you and give the option to save the entries or to remain in the current view.

The views bar is usually visible in every view. However, you can move the frame to change the size or hide it.
