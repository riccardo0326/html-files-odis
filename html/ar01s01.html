1. Introduction

Welcome to ODIS Creator Help!

This user guide provides insight into the function and operation of the ODIS Creator application. ODIS Creator is a part of the ODIS project ODIS (Offboard Diagnostic Information System), and the continued development of the vehicle diagnosis in the Volkswagen group will be conducted within its framework. The objective of the ODIS Creator subproject is to provide a communication platform between authoring locations around the world; with its help, the data exchange will be simpler and diagnostic preparation across groups and departments becomes possible.
