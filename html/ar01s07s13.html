7.13. Brand Variables

Variables can be declared for the separate brands under brand variables, which can be used across function tests. In the "Brand variables" navigator in the "editing" view, there is a corresponding brand node for every brand. The brand variables are located under the brand nodes and sorted alphanumerically in ascending order.
