6.1. Tasks

You switch to tasks by clicking on the Tasks action element in the Order management view. The "tasks" view is for editing coordinators, authors and translation coordinators when ODIS Creator opens after logging in. The respective tasks will be displayed to everyonein the form of orders,that are edited directly by your team or in the queue for your team. The tasks with the highest priority are all the way at the top. Furthermore, the system notifies the user about current changes to orders.
