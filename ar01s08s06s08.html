8.6.8. Completed (Externally)

This status indicates a successfully rebuilt baseline. The rebuilt data is in the folders defined by the administration. The exported objects as well as any object errors that occurred can be seen in the log files. The status is "Completed" in the status file.

The icon for this status shows a gear with a green checkmark.
