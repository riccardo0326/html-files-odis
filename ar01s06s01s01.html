6.1.1. Tasks for Editing Coordinators and Authors

The tasks for editing coordinators and authors consist of orders.

Authors and editing coordinators also see the orders which they are currently responsible for (see Section 6.2.3, “Order Status”). This is for authors that have not yet completed ad hoc orders for their own team as well as for scheduled orders with the "defining" status for the individual team, which have not reached their start date and are pending processing. You will also see all orders from the individual team in the "in processing" status. Through the usage locations dialog, you have the option to display the usage locations of the objects in the orders (see Section 6.2.4, “Usage Locations Dialog in Order Management”).

Based on their tasks, editing coordinators have a different view of the tasks. You will see the scheduled orders for a particular team with the status "defining", if the schedule must still be determined. You will also see all of the scheduled orders for the authoring teams at your location, which have past their scheduled start date and are still not currently being processed. You must clarify here why the processing has not started yet. Furthermore you will see all scheduled orders with the scheduled end date being the present day or in the past. If this is the case you will be informed of the scheduling delay in order to initiate the corresponding support measures.
