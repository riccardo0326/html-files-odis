3.3.1. Description of Roles

The separate roles are defined as follows.

Role / TeamDescription
Editing coordinatorAn editing coordinator is responsible for order management. He or she creates new orders (see Section 6.2, “Orders”) and order steps and decides to accept or reject them. The editing coordinator also commissions and distributes the task packages for processing the editorial objects to be created and is responsible for monitoring the completion deadlines. The editing coordinator is not responsible for the quality of the outcome.
Editing coordinator teamThe editing coordinators in an editing location are included in an editing coordinator team.
TeamThe addressee of an order step is always a team. The team manages the responsibility for a commissioned order step and the sole responsibility for the editorial objects to be edited. Currently the following teams are designated to edit order steps in ODIS Creator: authoring team, translation team.
AuthorAn author is responsible for processing editorial objects, that are assigned to them within the team. The author is responsible for the quality and the timely completion of the issue.
Authoring teamAn authoring team edits the editorial objects in the task area it is responsible for. Which author in the team works on a (commissioned) editorial object is decided independently by the members of the team.
Read-only authorA read-only author only has read-only access to the ODIS Creator system. The areas that are also available for authors can be viewed by read-only authors. The read-only authors can see objects that have the status "Confirmed completed" or "Released" in the editors, however they cannot change them. The function test editor is also completely usable (including ODIS debug). The read-only author can preview supplemental documents and XML templates as well as the display of values from a measured values table.
Read-only author teamA read-only author team includes one or more read-only authors and only has read-only access to the ODIS Creator system.
Translation coordinatorA translation coordinator is responsible for the translation management (see Section 6.4, “Translation”). Associated with this is the commissioning, scheduling and scope statement for the translation of editorial objects in ODIS Creator.
Translation coordinator teamThe translation coordinators are combined in a translation coordinator team.
EU5 export coordinatorAn EU5 export coordinator is responsible for the EU5 export (see Section 8.9, “EU5 Export”). Associated with this is the definition, validation and download of EU5 export logs in ODIS Creator.
EU5 export coordinator teamThe EU5 export coordinators are combined in an EU5 export coordinator team.
Text administrator

The text administrator manages default texts in ODIS Creator (see Section 5.5, “Default Texts”). The text administrator creates and edits text groups and default texts; they can deactivate or activate them.

The text administrator only available for the Wolfsburg location.

Text administrator teamThe ODIS Creator text administrators are combined in a text administrator team.
Location administratorA location administrator performs location-specific administration tasks for example, the creation of users (see Section 5.2.6, “Location Data”).
Administrator team locationThe ODIS Creator location administrators are combined in a location administrator team.
Group administratorA group administrator performs group-specific administration tasks (see Section 5.2.1, “Group Data”).
Group administrator teamThe ODIS Creator group administrators are combined in a group administrator team.
Cooperation export coordinatorA cooperation export coordinator is responsible for the cooperation export. This includes the definition, validation, and download of cooperation export logs in ODIS Creator.
Cooperation export coordinator teamThe cooperation export coordinators are combined in a cooperation export coordinator team.
InspectorA tester is responsible for Java box testing. This includes testing (accepting or declining) and completing the testing of Java boxes used in ODIS Creator.
Testing teamThe testers are combined in a testing team.
