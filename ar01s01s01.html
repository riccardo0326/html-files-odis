1.1. General Information

The main focus of the ODIS Creator application creates the editing system, which creates the diagnostic information, diagnostic analyses and function tests for the diagnostic tester. The aim of the vehicle diagnosis is the economical repair and servicing of high-tech vehicles, that have quick and clear conclusions about the cause of errors as well as the cost reduction in the production and customer service.

The editing system tasks are:

  • The provision of necessary information as well as an adequate work environment for the creation of diagnostic information by the authors.

  • Creation of fault finding programs for the vehicle diagnosis on the basis of the new standard test language as well as diagnostic information and diagnostic evaluations to use on diagnostic testers

  • Peripheral functions for the publication of information, for releases and management as well as for language and translation management, the administration and data exchange
