#### <a id="baseline.state.canceled"></a>8.6.3. Canceled

This status only applies to test baselines and shows that this test baseline was just canceled by the user. It may take some time until the cancellation actually occurs. After successfully canceling, the status will be set to [Error](8-6-4-error.md "8.6.4. Error").

The icon for this status shows ![](images/baseline_in_progress_cancel.png) a clock with a small, red exclamation point.
