### <a id="auftragsverwaltung.auftraege"></a>6.2. Orders

An order consists of one or more order steps. On the other hand, order steps consist of multiple **editorial objects**. To complete an order step, the contained editorial objects must be released individually or as a group (see [Section 6.2.5, “Releasing Editorial Objects”](6-2-5-releasing-editorial-objects.md "6.2.5. Releasing Editorial Objects")).

By selecting the **Orders** action element in the **Order management** [view](3-2-1-views-bar.md "3.2.1. Views Bar"), you will open the "Orders" view. Here all business transactions are displayed, that are required for the creation and monitoring of orders in connection with the processing of editorial objects for the editing coordinator and author roles. Orders consist of an order header<a id="d4e3867"></a>and of one or more order steps<a id="d4e3869"></a>. Order steps will be processed by only one team, order headers group the tasks for a specific theme.

In association with a scheduled market introduction, the creation of new editorial objects are generally initiated by [scheduled orders](6-2-1-scheduled-orders.md "6.2.1. Scheduled Orders")<a id="d4e3873"></a>. The creation of new editorial objects as well as the revision of already released editorial objects **after** the market introduction is done directly by the responsible author and can also be done outside of the scheduled orders. [Ad Hoc Orders](6-2-2-ad-hoc-orders.md "6.2.2. Ad Hoc Orders")<a id="d4e3877"></a>are created automatically as much as possible.
