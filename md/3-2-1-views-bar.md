#### <a id="perspektivenleiste"></a>3.2.1. Views Bar

All views for a specific topic are combined in ODIS Creator in **views**<a id="d4e665"></a>(for example [Figure 24, “Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator”](3-2-1-views-bar.md#figure.perspektivenleiste "Figure 24. Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator")). The individual views are opened through the [buttons](2-2-general-definitions-and-glossaries.md#schaltflaeche) in the view bar (such as [1a](3-2-1-views-bar.md#figure.perspektivenleiste "Figure 24. Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator") and [1b](3-2-1-views-bar.md#figure.perspektivenleiste "Figure 24. Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator")).

In the open views bar, all selectable action elements to open the individual [navigators](3-2-3-navigator.md "3.2.3. Navigator") are displayed.

In the **order management** view ([1a](3-2-1-views-bar.md#figure.perspektivenleiste "Figure 24. Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator")), you will find the action elements to display, for example, tasks and orders (see [Section 6, “ODIS Creator Order Management Process”](6-odis-creator-order-management-process.md "6. ODIS Creator Order Management Process")). If the (**Orders** action element [2](3-2-1-views-bar.md#figure.perspektivenleiste "Figure 24. Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator")) is selected, the order navigator ([3](3-2-1-views-bar.md#figure.perspektivenleiste "Figure 24. Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator")) is opened on the right side where the current orders are displayed.

<a id="figure.perspektivenleiste"></a>

![Image: views bar in ODIS Creator](images/Perspektivenleiste.png)

**Figure 24. Views Bar, Views and Action Elements in ODIS Creator**

You usually do not have to assume all tasks that can be performed in ODIS Creator. Therefore you will only see the views that are required for your work and research, and that correspond to your role in the system and the rights associated with that role.

If you switch to a different view, the [editors](3-2-5-editor.md "3.2.5. Editor") in the view you exited will close. If you have not saved your entries yet, ODIS Creator will ask you and give the option to save the entries or to remain in the current view.

The views bar is usually visible in every view. However, you can move the [frame](2-2-general-definitions-and-glossaries.md#rahmen) to change the size or hide it.
