## <a id="vaudes.gesch%C3%A4ftsprozesse"></a>4. ODIS Creator Business Processes

ODIS Creator is the editing system that allows diagnostic objects to be created and edited. These diagnostic objects are required for the [off-board diagnosis](11-appendix.md#offboard "Offboard") in the [ODIS Service](10-odis-creator-affiliated-systems.md#vaudas) processing system within "Guided Fault Finding" and "Guided Functions".
