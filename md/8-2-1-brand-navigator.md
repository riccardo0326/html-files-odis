#### <a id="marken.navigator"></a>8.2.1. Brand Navigator

All group brands are displayed in the brand navigator (see [Figure 604, “Brand Navigator”](8-2-1-brand-navigator.md#figure.produktiv.baseline.konfig.browser "Figure 604. Brand Navigator")). Only brands can be selected, which the location administrator has rights for. If the location administrator is only responsible for one brand, this will automatically be selected. After selecting the brands, the configuration editor will be displayed to the right of the brand navigator.
