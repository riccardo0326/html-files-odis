### <a id="rechte"></a>5.3. Rights

There are three different types of rights in ODIS Creator:

<a id="table.rechte"></a>

<table border="1" summary="Rights"><colgroup><col align="left"/><col align="left"/></colgroup><thead><tr bgcolor="#EEEEEE"><th align="left">Type of rights</th><th align="left">Description</th></tr></thead><tbody valign="top"><tr><td align="left" valign="top">Functional rights</td><td align="left" valign="top">Functional rights give the user the right to use controls on the application interface, for example a specific menu item and the function that is behind this menu item.</td></tr><tr><td align="left" valign="top">Object-specific rights</td><td align="left" valign="top">Object-specific rights are assigned to individual objects or object types and their attributes, and they give the user the right, for example read or write, to the whole object or one of its attributes.</td></tr><tr><td align="left" valign="top">Meta roles</td><td align="left" valign="top">Technical roles, such as the author, are represented by meta roles. This is not possible with the "normal" roles, since the administrator should be able to change them. Although meta roles do not have rights in the stricter sense of the word, they do have similar effects in the system and should therefore be handled at this location.</td></tr></tbody></table>
