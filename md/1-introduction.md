## <a id="einleitung"></a>1. Introduction

Welcome to ODIS Creator Help!

This user guide provides insight into the function and operation of the ODIS Creator application. ODIS Creator is a part of the ODIS project **ODIS** (**O**ffboard **D**iagnostic **I**nformation **S**ystem), and the continued development of the vehicle diagnosis in the Volkswagen group will be conducted within its framework. The objective of the ODIS Creator subproject is to provide a communication platform between authoring locations around the world; with its help, the data exchange will be simpler and diagnostic preparation across groups and departments becomes possible.
