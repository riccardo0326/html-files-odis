#### <a id="hilfe.starten"></a>2.1.1. Opening Help

ODIS Creator Help can be accessed through the **F1** button on the keyboard or in the [menu bar](2-2-general-definitions-and-glossaries.md#menuleiste) through **Help > Help text content** ([Figure 1, “Opening Help through the Menu Bar”](2-1-1-opening-help.md#figure.menuleiste.hilfe "Figure 1. Opening Help through the Menu Bar")).
