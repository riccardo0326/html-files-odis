#### <a id="Funktionstesteditor.Allgemeine_Funktionalitaet.Kontextmen%C3%BC"></a>7.14.22. Context menu

By clicking on the right mouse button, a context menu can be opened, in which the included items will be arranged after the position of the cursor. See [Figure 540, “Context menu”](7-14-22-context-menu.md#figure.Funktionstesteditor.Allgemeine_Funktionalitaet.Kontextmenue "Figure 540. Context menu").
