### <a id="prozess.bereitstellung.eu5export"></a>8.9. EU5 Export

EU5 exports are compilations of Guided Functions that must also be made available to independent workshops in accordance with EU regulation (715/2007 and 692/2008 of the EU commission).

The view for creating and viewing EU5 exports is divided into three different displays (see [Figure 612, “EU5 Export Display”](8-9-eu5-export.md#figure.eu5export.overview "Figure 612. EU5 Export Display")). The navigation tree and the properties display on the left side, the editor to define the EU5 exports on the right side. The displays are described in more detail in the following.

<a id="figure.eu5export.overview"></a>

![Image: EU5 export display](images/eu5export.png)

**Figure 612. EU5 Export Display**

Similar to the production/test baselines, the results of EU5 exports are saved automatically to a centrally defined folder. The results in the central folder are deleted if possible when the EU5 export is deleted in OC.
